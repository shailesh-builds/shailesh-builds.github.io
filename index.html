About

I explain things well.
That is my superpower.

I am a Senior Product Manager with 16 years of experience — most of it spent building and scaling mission-critical enterprise platforms used by tens of thousands of professionals across 150+ countries, in one of the most regulated domains in global professional services.

I am deeply inspired by the modern product mindset — the belief that the best teams don't just build features, they solve problems. They measure outcomes, not output. They talk to real users, not just stakeholders. They ship thin slices to learn, rather than big batches to guess. This is the model I live by, coach, and build tooling around.

Beyond my day-to-day product work, I spend significant time coaching teams through the shift from traditional delivery to discovery-led ways of working — and building AI-powered tools that make that shift faster and more durable. I also write and build in public, because explaining things clearly is the skill I am most proud of.

Product
teardowns

A teardown is not a review. It is a structured analysis of how a product creates value — the decisions behind what exists, what is missing, and what could be better. One product at a time.

Framework
How I do a teardown
Every teardown follows the same structure — user, problem, business model, moat, PM problem framing, and what I would change. Consistent lens, honest analysis, research-backed throughout.
